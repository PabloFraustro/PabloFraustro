Kuniyoshi art exhibition




Based on traditional woodblock prints by Utagawa Kuniyoshi. The concept project includes: physical installation, projection content and interactive machines.


My main role in the project included exhibition structure and video content.

The exhibition walks visitors through an experience starting from mystic foggy landscapes to a battlefield scenario, ending on an interactive “create your woodbloock” area.
