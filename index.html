My life balance, my hobbie and mental/physical training.

How can technology help humankind?


This revolutionized digital world has more to offer than memory expansions, great graphics and connected leisure.

Communicating with computers is the first part of this revolution, the next phase requires human interaction, and is there where I plan to contribute to this world. Human interaction is expressed not just with 8-bit codified characters, it is based on emotions expressed on art, movement, gestures, silences and interaction with objects. Let us make use of this technology to express ourselves beyond time and space, let us connect among humans.
